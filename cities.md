# Host Cities & Stadiums

Morocco has several cities capable of hosting AFCON matches.

## Casablanca
- Largest city in Morocco
- Strong football culture

## Rabat
- Capital city
- Modern infrastructure

## Marrakech
- Famous tourist destination
- Rich cultural history

## Tangier
- Coastal city
- Modern and international
